Booking a professional cleaning for the first time can feel like a big step. Maybe you've never had someone else clean your home before. Maybe you're a little nervous about letting someone new through the front door. We get it — and we want you to know that's completely normal.

## It All Starts With a Conversation

Before we ever show up at your door in Logan, UT, we like to get to know you a little bit. When you reach out — whether by phone, text, or through our website — we'll have a quick, relaxed chat about your home and what matters most to you.

We'll ask about things like:

- **The size of your home** — How many bedrooms and bathrooms are we working with?
- **Your priorities** — Is the kitchen driving you crazy? Are the bathrooms your biggest headache?
- **Any special requests** — Do you have pets? Allergies? Areas you'd like us to skip or focus on?
- **Your schedule** — We'll find a day and time that works seamlessly with your life.

There's no cookie-cutter approach here. Every home in Cache Valley is different, and every family has different needs. We build your cleaning plan around *you*, not the other way around.

## What Happens on Cleaning Day

When our team arrives at your home, you'll notice something right away — our heroes are friendly. Genuinely friendly. We're not going to rush past you with a clipboard and a stiff handshake. We'll introduce ourselves, ask if there's anything you'd like us to know, and then get to work.

Here's a general idea of what a first visit covers:

- **Kitchen** — Countertops wiped down, appliance exteriors cleaned, sink scrubbed, floors mopped, and all those little crumbs and splatters taken care of.
- **Bathrooms** — Toilets, showers, tubs, mirrors, and vanities all get thorough attention. We tackle the grime so you don't have to.
- **Living areas and bedrooms** — Dusting, vacuuming, tidying surfaces, and making everything feel fresh and welcoming.
- **Floors throughout** — Vacuumed, swept, or mopped depending on the surface.

Your first cleaning may take a little longer than future visits, and that's by design. We want to give your home a strong, thorough starting point. Think of it as resetting the slate so that every visit after is quicker and easier to maintain.
