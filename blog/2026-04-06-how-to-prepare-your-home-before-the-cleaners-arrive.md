# How to Prepare Your Home Before the Cleaners Arrive

You've booked a professional cleaning — congratulations! You're about to take the load off and come home to a fresh, sparkling space. But before your cleaning heroes arrive, there are a few easy things you can do to help them make the most of every minute in your home.

At Hero Cleaners, we've cleaned over 7,800 homes right here in Cache Valley, and we've learned that a little bit of prep goes a long way. These tips aren't about doing our job for us — they're about helping us do our job even better so you can truly relax.

## Pick Up Personal Items and Clutter

Here's the thing: professional cleaners are there to clean surfaces, not organize your stuff. When countertops, floors, and tables are covered in personal items, toys, mail, or laundry, your cleaning team has to work around those things instead of diving straight into the deep scrubbing and sanitizing you're paying for.

Before your cleaning appointment, take a quick walk through each room and:

- **Put away loose clothing and shoes** — toss them in a hamper or closet
- **Clear countertops** of mail, paperwork, and random items
- **Pick up kids' toys** and put them in a bin or basket
- **Move any valuables or fragile items** to a safe spot

This doesn't have to be a big production. Even 10-15 minutes of quick tidying makes a huge difference. Families all across Logan UT tell us they're amazed at how much more thorough the clean feels when they declutter beforehand.

## Handle the Dishes

We know — nobody loves dishes. But a sink full of dirty plates and pans can slow things down. If your cleaning package includes kitchen deep cleaning, your team needs access to the sink and surrounding counters to scrub, sanitize, and make everything shine.

You don't have to hand-wash every dish. Just load up the dishwasher or stack things neatly to one side. That small step frees your cleaners to focus on the things that really matter: degreasing stovetops, wiping down cabinets, and making your kitchen sparkle from top to bottom.

## Make Room for Access

Think about the areas you'd like cleaned and make sure your team can get to them easily. A few things that help:

- **Move chairs away from tables** if you'd like the dining area detailed
- **Clear shower shelves** of personal products so we can scrub tile and grout
- **Open interior doors** so no room gets accidentally skipped
- **Ensure access to cleaning areas** like laundry rooms, mudrooms, and entryways

If you have rooms that are off-limits or areas you'd prefer we skip, just let us know. We respect your space and your boundaries — always.

## Secure Pets and Let Us Know About Allergies

We love Cache Valley pets — from the golden retrievers bounding through North Logan parks to the barn cats out in Wellsville. But for your pet's comfort (and your cleaners' safety), it's best to secure animals in a safe space during the cleaning.

Also, if anyone in your household has allergies or sensitivities to certain products, give us a heads-up. We're happy to accommodate special requests so everyone in your home stays comfortable and healthy.

## Don't Stress About the Mess

Here's the most important tip of all: **don't apologize for your mess.** Seriously. We've seen it all, and we're never here to judge. Whether your home in Logan UT needs a quick refresh or a full deep clean after a long Cache Valley winter, that's exactly what we're here for.

You hired us so you don't have to carry the weight of keeping every surface perfect. We clean, you relax — that's the deal.
