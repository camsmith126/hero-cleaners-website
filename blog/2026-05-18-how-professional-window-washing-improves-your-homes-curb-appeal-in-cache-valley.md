## Cache Valley's Climate Is Tough on Windows

Living in Cache Valley means experiencing all four seasons in vivid detail. That's one of the things we love about this place. But each season brings its own challenges for your windows:

- **Spring** brings pollen, cottonwood fluff, and rain spots that coat glass surfaces almost overnight.
- **Summer** kicks up dust from dry roads and nearby fields, especially in communities like Nibley, Wellsville, and North Logan.
- **Fall** means leaves, sap, and early frost that can leave stubborn marks.
- **Winter** delivers road salt spray, ice melt residue, and moisture that leads to mineral staining.

By scheduling professional window washing at least once or twice a year, you stay ahead of the seasonal grime and keep your home looking its best no matter the time of year.

## The Hidden Benefits Beyond Looks

Curb appeal is the obvious win, but clean windows actually offer a few benefits you might not expect:

**More natural light.** Dirty windows can block a surprising amount of sunlight. Clean glass lets more light flood your rooms, making your home feel warmer and more open — which is especially welcome during Cache Valley's darker winter months.

**Longer window lifespan.** Hard water deposits and mineral buildup can actually etch into glass over time, causing permanent damage. Regular professional cleaning protects your investment and keeps your windows in great condition for years.

**Better energy efficiency.** When your windows are clean and their seals are in good shape, they perform better at insulating your home. During our cleaning process, we often notice cracked seals or damaged weatherstripping that homeowners weren't aware of.

**A mood boost.** This one's simple. Looking through crystal-clear windows at the Bear River Mountains or a fresh Cache Valley snowfall just *feels* good. Your home should be a place that lifts your spirits, and clean windows are a small thing that makes a big difference.
