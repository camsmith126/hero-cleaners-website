# What Does a Professional House Cleaner Actually Clean? A Room-by-Room Breakdown

You've been thinking about hiring a professional house cleaner. Maybe the weekends are too short, the kids are too busy, or you're just plain tired of scrubbing the same baseboards every other Saturday. We get it — and honestly, that's exactly why we started Hero Cleaners right here in Logan, UT.

But before you book, you probably have a very reasonable question: *What exactly does a professional house cleaner do when they show up at your door?*

Great question. Let's walk through it room by room so you know exactly what to expect — and maybe get a little excited about coming home to a sparkling clean house without lifting a finger.

## The Kitchen: Where the Magic (and the Mess) Happens

The kitchen is the heart of every Cache Valley home, and it's usually the room that needs the most love. Here's what our team tackles:

- **Countertops and backsplash** — wiped down, sanitized, and free of crumbs and sticky spots
- **Stovetop and range hood** — grease, splatters, and buildup removed
- **Exterior of appliances** — refrigerator, dishwasher, oven, and microwave wiped clean
- **Inside the microwave** — yes, even the crusty spaghetti explosion from last Tuesday
- **Sink and faucet** — scrubbed, polished, and shining
- **Cabinet fronts** — wiped down to remove fingerprints and grime
- **Floors** — swept, mopped, and looking brand new
- **Trash emptied** — because nobody should come home to a full trash can

If you're booking a deep clean, we'll go even further — cleaning inside the oven, scrubbing grout, and pulling out appliances to clean behind them.

## Bathrooms: From Grimy to Gleaming

Let's be honest — cleaning the bathroom is nobody's favorite chore. That's exactly why people in Logan, UT and across Cache Valley call us. Here's what we handle:

- **Toilet** — inside, outside, base, and behind (yes, behind)
- **Shower and tub** — soap scum, mildew, and hard water stains scrubbed away
- **Sink and vanity** — cleaned, sanitized, and polished
- **Mirrors** — streak-free and sparkling
- **Countertops** — cleared and wiped down
- **Floors** — swept and mopped, including around the base of the toilet
- **Fixtures and hardware** — faucets, towel bars, and handles shined up
- **Trash emptied**

Our heroes know that the bathroom is where you really notice the difference. There's nothing like stepping into a freshly cleaned bathroom after a long day.

## Bedrooms: Your Personal Retreat

Your bedroom should feel like a sanctuary, not a to-do list. Here's what professional cleaning looks like in the bedroom:

- **Bed made and linens straightened** — we won't change sheets unless requested, but we'll make everything look neat and inviting
- **Dusting** — nightstands, dressers, shelves, lamps, and ceiling fans
- **Floors** — vacuumed (including under the bed when accessible) or mopped
- **Mirrors and glass surfaces** — wiped streak-free
- **Baseboards** — dusted or wiped down
- **Light switches and door handles** — sanitized

It's the kind of room-by-room attention that makes coming home at the end of the day feel like a mini vacation.

## Living Areas and Common Spaces

The living room, family room, entryway, hallways — these high-traffic areas collect dust, pet hair, and everyday clutter faster than you'd think. Here's how we take the load off:

- **Dusting** — shelves, entertainment centers, mantels, picture frames, and décor
- **Couch cushions** — straightened and fluffed (not deep-cleaned, but tidied)
- **Coffee tables and end tables** — wiped and dusted
- **Floors** — vacuumed, swept, or mopped depending on the surface
- **Baseboards and corners** — dusted and wiped
- **Light fixtures and ceiling fans** — dusted
- **Windows sills and ledges** — wiped clean
- **Light switches and door handles** — sanitized

If you have pets — and we know many Cache Valley families do — we pay extra attention to pet hair on floors, furniture edges, and those sneaky corners where fur loves to gather.

## What About Extras?

Every home is different, and that's something we genuinely love about serving families across Logan, UT and the surrounding communities. Beyond the standard room-by-room clean, here are some extras you can add:

- **Interior window washing**
- **Inside the refrigerator or oven**
- **Laundry folding**
- **Organizing spaces**
- **Garage or basement tidying**
- **Move-out deep cleans** — perfect for renters and landlords in Cache Valley

We build every cleaning plan around your home, your priorities, and your schedule. No cookie-cutter packages here.

## What We Don't Clean

In the spirit of being upfront and honest (that's kind of our thing), here are a few things that typically fall outside a standard cleaning visit:

- **Biohazard or hoarding situations**
- **Exterior power washing**
- **Carpet shampooing** (we vacuum like pros, though)
- **Heavy furniture moving**

If you're ever unsure whether something is included, just ask. We're happy to chat and figure out exactly what you need.
