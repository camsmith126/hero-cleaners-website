What Goes Into Your Price

EVERY HOME IS DIFFERENT — SO IS EVERY PRICE

We don't price cleanings off a generic checklist. We shape the scope to your home, the cadence to your life, and the price to what you actually need.

HOME SIZE

Bigger homes take longer. We factor in square footage, bedrooms, and bathrooms when we quote.

FREQUENCY

Recurring customers see a lower per-visit price than one-time cleans — we maintain a baseline instead of resetting from scratch every time.

SCOPE

What matters most to you shapes what we focus on. Pets, kids, a deep-clean reset, special requests — we build it into the quote.

  • We communicate. We answer the phone. We tell you what to expect before we show up.
  • If something isn't right, we come back and fix it — no $15 credit, no argument.

People pay for a clean. They stay for the people who show up every week and learn your home like it's theirs.
