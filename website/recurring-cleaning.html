Not Sure Which Plan?

WE'LL HELP YOU FIGURE IT OUT

A lot of people aren't sure which frequency is right when they first book. That's completely normal — and it's exactly why we reach out before your first visit.

We'll talk through your home, how it gets used, what matters most to you, and what fits your budget. After that conversation, we make a real recommendation — not a guess.

For what it's worth: if you're torn between bi-weekly and monthly, bi-weekly is almost always the better call. The starting price difference is real. The difference in how your home feels is even more real.

What's Included

BUILT AROUND YOUR HOME

We don't hand every customer the same checklist and call it done. What gets cleaned, how thoroughly, and in what order depends on you — your home, your priorities, and what matters most to your family.

That's why we keep communication open — before your first visit and throughout our time together. Before we ever show up, we have a real conversation about what you care about most — whether that's the kitchen, the bathrooms, the floors, or something specific to your household.

Over time, your cleaner gets to know your home and your preferences without you having to repeat yourself. That's what makes recurring cleaning with Hero Cleaners actually work.
