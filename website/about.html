Our Story

HOW TWO COLLEGE KIDS BECAME CACHE VALLEY'S #1

Hero Cleaners started in 2019 with nothing but a bucket, a squeegee, and a belief that people in Cache Valley deserved better than the average cleaning company — one that was impersonal, inconsistent, and treated customers like transactions.

We wanted to show up reliably, do the work right, and build real relationships. Not just clean houses, but genuinely make people's lives easier. That vision hasn't changed since day one.

Today we're Cache Valley's most trusted cleaning service — serving over 500 customers across 12 cities, with a team of people who genuinely care about the work they do and the homes they clean.
