HOW WE SERVE EACH CITY

LOGAN, UT

Logan is our home base. Most of our residential and commercial customers are right here in Logan, Utah, and we run routes across the city every day. From downtown neighborhoods to the USU area, from River Heights to the Island, we know Logan inside and out. Weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, deep cleans, and commercial — whatever you need, we’re already close by.

NORTH LOGAN, UT

Hero Cleaners has been serving North Logan homes and businesses since 2019. Recurring maid service, deep cleans, and commercial cleaning for offices and retail — we cover all of North Logan, Utah with the same local team every visit. North Logan is one of our busiest service areas alongside Logan itself.

SMITHFIELD, UT

Smithfield is one of our top service areas in north Cache Valley. We run regular routes through Smithfield, Utah every week for recurring maid service customers, plus deep cleans, move-in/move-out cleans, and commercial work. Same cleaner every visit — a real relationship, not a rotating crew.

HYRUM, UT

Hyrum homes and businesses trust Hero Cleaners for recurring house cleaning, deep cleans, and commercial service. Whether you’re near the reservoir or in the heart of town, we’ll build a schedule that fits your life. Residential and commercial both welcome.

NIBLEY, UT

We serve all of Nibley, Utah with weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly recurring cleaning, as well as deep cleans and move-out service. Nibley is growing fast, and we’re growing with it — a reliable local cleaning team for every new home and every long-time resident.

PROVIDENCE, UT

Providence is home to many of our recurring maid service customers. From small cottages to larger family homes, we tailor each visit to what matters most to you. Licensed, insured, and locally owned — the way Providence families like it.

HYDE PARK, UT

Hyde Park residents count on Hero Cleaners for consistent, trustworthy house cleaning. Same cleaner every visit, no-contract flexibility, and a team that actually shows up when they say they will. Recurring, deep clean, and move-out service all available.

WELLSVILLE, UT

Wellsville, Utah is on our regular Cache Valley service route. Recurring cleaning, deep cleans, and seasonal window washing — we bring the same care to Wellsville that we bring to every other city in the valley. Call us to set up your schedule.

RICHMOND, UT

Richmond is one of our north-valley service areas. Recurring maid service, deep cleans, and commercial cleaning for local businesses. We’ll drive up, do the work right, and leave your home or business looking its best.

RIVER HEIGHTS, UT

River Heights is right next door to our Logan home base, which means we’re often there already. Recurring cleaning, deep cleans, move-in/move-out service — whatever your home needs, we’re a few minutes away.

LEWISTON, UT

Lewiston is at the north end of Cache Valley, and we serve it by appointment. Deep cleans, recurring schedules, and move-out service all available — give us a call to set up a visit and we’ll work out the best schedule for your home.

CLARKSTON, UT

Clarkston is one of our smaller service areas — we cover it by appointment. If you’re in Clarkston and need cleaning service, call us and we’ll figure out a schedule that works. We’re locally owned and we believe every Cache Valley community deserves the same care.
