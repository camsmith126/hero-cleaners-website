MAID SERVICE FAQS

WHAT’S THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN A MAID SERVICE AND HOUSE CLEANING?

Honestly? Not much. “Maid service” usually implies recurring visits with the same cleaner, while “house cleaning” can be one-time or recurring. At Hero Cleaners, our maid service is our recurring cleaning — weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly visits with a consistent cleaner you actually know.

DO I GET THE SAME MAID EVERY VISIT?

Yes — that’s the whole point. We match you with a cleaner and keep it that way. If your regular cleaner is ever out, we’ll let you know ahead of time and introduce you to who’s filling in.

DO I NEED TO SIGN A CONTRACT?

Never. Our maid service in Logan runs on trust, not contracts. Skip a week, pause for a month, or cancel anytime — no fees, no guilt trips.

IS MY HOUSE TOO MESSY FOR A MAID SERVICE?

No judgment, ever. We’ve seen it all — and the reason people hire us is because they don’t want to worry about it. That’s our job.

ARE YOUR MAIDS INSURED?

Yes. Every cleaner on our team is licensed, insured, and background-checked. Your home and your peace of mind are always covered.
