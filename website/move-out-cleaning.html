What We Cover

EVERY SURFACE A WALK-THROUGH WILL CHECK

A move-out clean is the detail clean — every surface, inside every cabinet and appliance, every baseboard and corner. The work a regular tidy doesn't reach is exactly what a walk-through flags.

  • Inside ovens, refrigerators, microwaves, and dishwashers
  • Inside cabinets, drawers, and pantry shelves
  • All baseboards, door frames, light switches, and outlet covers
  • Bathrooms top to bottom — tile, grout, fixtures, behind toilets
  • Window sills, tracks, and inside-glass cleaning
  • Ceiling fans, vents, blinds, and light fixtures
  • Floors mopped, vacuumed, or steam-cleaned as the floor type calls for
  • Walls spot-cleaned where needed (we'll flag damage that needs more than cleaning)
