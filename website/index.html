Our Story

BORN IN CACHE VALLEY. BUILT ON TRUST.

Two college buddies started Hero Cleaners in 2019 with nothing but a bucket, a squeegee, and a belief that people deserve better than the average cleaning company.

We wanted to be heroes for our neighbors — showing up reliably, doing the work right, and building relationships that actually meant something. That vision hasn't changed.

Today we're Cache Valley's most trusted cleaning service, and every job we take is still personal to us.
