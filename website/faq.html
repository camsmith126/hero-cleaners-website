No contracts. We believe we should earn your business every visit — not lock you into something. Recurring customers can pause, adjust, or cancel anytime.
We store a card securely on file and charge after each completed visit. No invoices, no chasing payments — it's handled automatically so you never have to think about it. We do require a card on file to book.
